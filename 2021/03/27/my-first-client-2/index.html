My first client

Leave a Comment / Uncategorized / By Ritika

I was nervous yet excited to take my first call with the client. I had worked days to get this project. Thankfully, my client believed in my skills and passion and hired me to build an e-commerce website for her online store. I still remember that excitement when she was giving me the details she wanted to have on her website. I did not sleep the whole night and kept experimenting with different themes and layouts. I completed the project days earlier than she expected and she was impressed with my design.
